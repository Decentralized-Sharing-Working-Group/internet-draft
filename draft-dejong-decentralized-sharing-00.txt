                         Decentralized Sharing

Abstract

s draft describes what are the steps and challenges to achieve an interoperable decentralized private sharing between users having a personal server. This sharing should be feasible no matter what is the platform running on the server, as long as it capable of storing and processing data and that the user has control on it.
